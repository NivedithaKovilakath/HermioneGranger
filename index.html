About

I am a Muggle-born witch, and I went to Hogwarts when the anti-muggleborn sentiment was on the rise.
I was bullied, and called slurs throughout my childhood.
So, I have made it my life's mission to ensure that no other child has to face injustice like I did.

I graduated from Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1998,
where I was sorted into Griffindor.
I received ten OWLs and seven NEWTs for my academic performance.
I was also a prefect during my time at the school.
I joined the Ministry of Magic right after I graduated from Hogwarts.
I worked for the Department of Magical Law Enforcement, where I played a big part in
eliminating laws biased against muggle-born witches and wizards.
I have also been an advocate for the rights of house elves.
I became the Minister of Magic in 2019.
