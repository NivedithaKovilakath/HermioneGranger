About

I am an English Muggle-born witch, and my parents were both Dentists. I have made it my life's mission to fight against muggle-born injustice and to promote
Elfish welfare.

I graduated from Hogwarts School of
Witchcraft and Wizardry in 1998,
where I was sorted into Griffindor.
I received ten OWLs and seven NEWTs for
my academic performance.
I was also a prefect during my time at school.

Work

I was one of the founders of Dumbledore's Army and help fight against Lord Voldemort
I joined the Ministry of Magic right after I graduated from Hogwarts.
I worked for the Department of Magical Law Enforcement, where I played a big part in eliminating laws biased against muggle-born witches and wizards.
I have also been an advocate for the rights of house elves
and founded The Society for Promotion of Elfish Welfare(S.P.E.W) when I was at Hogwarts.
I became the Minister of Magic in 2019.
